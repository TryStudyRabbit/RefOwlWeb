Frequently Asked Questions

Is RefOwl really free?

Yes! RefOwl is completely free to use with no hidden fees or premium features.

Which citation styles are supported?

Currently we support APA (7th Edition), MLA (9th Edition), and Chicago (17th Edition). We plan to add more styles in future updates.

Does it work for all types of sources?

RefOwl currently supports web pages, books, journal articles, and newspaper articles. We're constantly adding more source types.

How accurate is the auto-fill feature?

Our auto-fill technology is highly accurate for most academic websites, journals, and news sites. You can always review and edit any fields before generating your citation.

Where is my citation data stored?

All your saved citations are stored locally in your browser. We don't collect or store any of your citation data on our servers.

Can I export my citations to a Word document?

Yes! You can export all your saved citations as a formatted bibliography that you can paste directly into your paper.
